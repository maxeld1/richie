MEET THE TEAM

Mary Beth

Level 8
Owner / Stylist / Educator

Industry’s owner Mary Beth is a passionate hairstylist, wife, and mom of three, living and raising her family in the same community of her salon. With over 20 years of experience, hair has always been more than a career—it’s a way to build trust, create meaningful connections, and help clients feel confident as the best version of themselves.

She specializes in customized color, effortless lived-in styles, and wedding and formal styling, with a strong focus on detail and intentional work. After years behind the chair, Mary Beth created a salon space that reflects what she values most: a relaxed, welcoming environment rooted in quality, continued education, and genuine care.

Every appointment is about more than great hair—it’s about confidence, comfort, and making each client feel truly seen.

Lisa

Level 7
Co-Founder / Stylist / Educator

As a co-founder, Level 7 Stylist, and Redken Artist, Lisa brings extensive expertise and passion to every guest experience. With advanced training in cutting, coloring, styling, and wedding hair, Lisa specializes in creating customized looks that enhance each guest’s individuality and lifestyle.

Certified in Redken Hair Color and Design (cutting), and recognized as a Redken Specialist, Lisa has worked on Redken photoshoots and commercials and travels nationally to educate and inspire other salons through Redken education. Each year, she also attends ongoing trainings in NYC, LA, and Las Vegas to stay inspired and ahead of trends.

At the heart of her work, she believes in delivering an exceptional guest experience—one that is personalized, elevated, and fun. Every service is thoughtfully tailored to meet each guest’s needs while creating an atmosphere that is both professional and enjoyable.
